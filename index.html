Problem Statement

FlashFit addresses two major clothing challenges: online shopping lacks the ability to try on clothes before purchase, leading to high return rates and customer dissatisfaction, while daily outfit selection wastes valuable time. Instead of spending minutes changing clothes every morning, FlashFit provides instant virtual try-on capabilities for both online shopping and daily wardrobe decisions.

Innovative Solution

FlashFit bridges this gap by providing a dual-platform virtual fitting system that uses advanced computer vision to enable real-time clothing visualization on both mobile devices and smart mirrors.
